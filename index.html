About us

The Elimu Foundation supports the provision of education opportunities to the most disadvantaged communities across the world. We do this by developing forward thinking programmes and working collaboratively with local partners to meet the needs of each community we work with.

Our Programmes

Our Flagship programme Elimu:Tech is a technology programme which aims to give young people technology skills for the 21st century. We give young people an introduction into the world of technology and teach them web design, web development, programming etc.

Working with local partners, we are currently initiating the programme in West and Southern Africa and are looking to expand our reach. To find out more about our work, please do get in touch.

Partnering with us

We believe in partnerships! Working together to meet a goal. To partner with us or to find out more about what we do please contact us.
